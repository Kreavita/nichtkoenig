Nichtkoenig Rules:

This round-based card game is about lying and detecting lies. A player starts with 2 random cards from a specific skat set. These cards represent the lives of the player and a card can be slain in three ways: an opponent uses 7 (3 with ace) stones to slay a card, an opponent rightfully accuses the last action of the player as impossible, or the player wrongly accuses the last action of an opponent as impossible. Besides that, there are three actions a player can perform every round:
  • Take stones. (1 or 2, 3 with King)
  • Attack someone with 7 stones. (3 with Ace)
  • Perform a card action:
